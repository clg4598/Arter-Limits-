About Arter Limits


Welcome to Arter Limits, where creativity knows no bounds and artistic expression finds its true home. Nestled at the intersection of imagination and innovation, Arter Limits is more than just a gallery; it's a thriving community of artists and art enthusiasts united by a passion for pushing the boundaries of creativity.


Our gallery showcases a diverse tapestry of artistic genres, from spine-tingling horror to awe-inspiring fantasy and mind-bending surrealism. At Arter Limits, we believe that art is a journey into the extraordinary, and our curated collection reflects the boundless possibilities of the human imagination.

What sets Arter Limits apart is not only the exceptional artwork on display but also the vibrant atmosphere created by our community. We are proud to be a melting pot of talent, drawing artists and art lovers from across the state of Mississippi and beyond. Our collective is a testament to the power of creativity to transcend geographical boundaries, bringing together individuals who share a common love for the arts.

But Arter Limits is more than just a visual spectacle; it's a multisensory experience. Prepare to be serenaded by the soul-stirring tunes of live music that resonate through our gallery space. Our commitment to creating a holistic artistic environment extends beyond the canvas, embracing the auditory senses with performances that complement the visual feast before your eyes.

Whether you are an artist seeking inspiration, a collector in search of the next masterpiece, or simply an art enthusiast looking to immerse yourself in a vibrant community, Arter Limits welcomes you. Join us in celebrating the limitless possibilities of artistic expression and become a part of a community that thrives on pushing the boundaries of creativity.

Our Mission


MISSION: At Arter Limits, our mission is to redefine the artistic landscape, bringing forth a dynamic fusion of edgy and imaginative expressions that resonate with every taste. As the first of its kind in the state of Mississippi, Arter Limits stands as a pioneering force, a beacon illuminating the diverse spectrum of voices and visions that thrive within our community.


VISION: In a region steeped in tradition, Arter Limits emerges as a bold departure, showcasing an unprecedented gathering of artists and art lovers who dare to explore the uncharted realms of creativity. Our commitment lies in providing a platform for those whose art defies convention, offering an eclectic blend that challenges norms and captivates the senses.

What sets Arter Limits apart is the emphasis on art that speaks not only to the individual but to society at large. We believe in the transformative power of art, a force that can shape perceptions, challenge preconceptions, and spark conversations. Our gallery serves as a canvas for artists with a story to tell, a message to convey from the depths of their hearts and minds.

Imagination and creativity reign supreme at Arter Limits. We celebrate the unbridled potential of the human imagination, encouraging artists to delve into the recesses of their creativity and bring forth works that leave an indelible mark. It is a space where the unconventional is embraced, where artistic boundaries are shattered, and where every stroke of the brush or note of music is an assertion of individuality.

At Arter Limits, we invite you to be part of a movement that transcends artistic limitations. Whether you are an artist looking to push the boundaries of your craft or an art enthusiast seeking a journey into unexplored realms, Arter Limits welcomes you.
